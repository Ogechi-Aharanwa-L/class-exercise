About Me

I am a detail oriented professional with a background in financial services operations and experience in customer service management. This experience strengthened my ability to manage processes, solve problems under pressure, and deliver consistent service quality. I am naturally drawn to structured environments and continuous improvement, with a strong interest in finance, productivity, and understanding how systems work behind the scenes.

My interest in technology developed from wanting to better understand the digital systems that support modern financial services. This led me to begin learning web development, starting with HTML and CSS, where I focus on building a solid foundation in structure and design. I am motivated by the need to create practical, user focused solutions and to transition into a field where I can combine analytical thinking with technical skills.

Outside of work and learning, I enjoy exploring digital tools, watching insightful content, and organizing ideas in a way that improves how I think and work. I am particularly interested in how people interact with technology and how small design decisions can shape user experience. This curiosity continues to drive my growth as I develop both my technical skills and problem solving approach.
